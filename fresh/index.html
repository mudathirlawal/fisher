About Us

Company Profile

Nxy Geo-Prudential Concept Ltd is an independent and wholly indigenous Nigerian company. The company was formed by a group of professionals to provide professional and specialized services especially to the Surveying, Engineering and Project Management as a way of contributing to industrialization of Nigeria. The company, with registered office in Lagos, is fast becoming a major competitor in the Surveying and Engineering Industry.

Our core commitment is to provide safe product and solutions for multifaceted Surveying and Engineering Services using High-tech Precision Positioning, and motion measurement.


Key Personnel

Our team comprise of well experienced profesional surveyors, with some having long-term experience, acquired through working both in public and private sectors of the profession.

Quadri Sadiq

Managing Director

Sadiq, a registered surveyor & GIS expert, who holds MSc in Surveying & Geoinformatics from Unilag. Sadiq is also a government surveyor who has a wide range of experience in GIS, Geodecy & Cadastral Surveying. He is a dynamic and amiable member of Nxy Geo-Prudentials Ltd.

Shitta-Bey Olabode

Executive Chairman

A seasoned government surveyor with an outstanding 22 years of experience; a professional marketer who has a strong acumen for entrepreneurship and professionalism. Mallam Shitta-Bey is also a planner and GIS expert of high repute. He is a prolific writer and the CEO of Nxy Geo-Prudentials Ltd.

Adegbite Usman

ED Operations/Projects

An experienced geomatician and first class graduate of Federal School of Surveying, Oyo, the citadel of geomatics education in Sub-saharan African. Usman has successfully executed projects in highly technical areas of the surveying field such as hydrographic surveying, engineering surveying and cadastral surveying.
